## Hellfire Harry Needs At Least 2 Targets To Attack

"Hellfire Harry" has a requirement that in order for the attack execution to be considered successful in the planning phase, it needs to be able to target 2 enemies. If you space characters out enough, he just does nothing and he won't even bother shooting at just 1 enemy.
